The following images show how fish have been affected by pollution.

Human pollution has a significant impact on the sea life. The dumping of waste, chemicals, plastics, and other harmful materials into the oceans disrupts the delicate balance of the marine ecosystem. The marine animals are particularly affected, with many species ingesting or getting entangled in plastic debris, leading to injury, suffocation, and death. The chemicals and toxins in the water can also accumulate in the tissues of marine animals, leading to serious health problems and even death. Overfishing, oil spills, and other forms of pollution also contribute to the decline in marine populations. The harmful effects of human pollution on the ocean's wildlife are an urgent concern, requiring immediate and sustained action to address this crisis..
